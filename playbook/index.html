What is Quanto?

Quanto is a manual expense tracker and budgeting app for iOS and Android. It's beautifully designed and built for people who want real clarity on where their money goes.

How does it work?

Add your transactions manually and Quanto organizes everything — charts, summaries, categories — so you always know where your money is going.
