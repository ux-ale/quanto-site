Step 5

Film the videos

  1. Open Quanto on the Activity page.
  2. Swipe on the main chart until you reach the month that shows all the transactions.
  3. While Quanto is open, lock the iPhone.
  4. Before filming, unlock the iPhone once off camera so you do not need to unlock it during the video.
  5. With the second phone, start recording.
  6. Show the object you are about to track for at least 1 second.
  7. Bring the iPhone into the frame.
  8. Turn the iPhone around to show the back of the phone.
  9. Double tap the back of the iPhone.
  10. Turn the iPhone to the front to show that the double tap shortcut was activated.
  11. Input the amount.
  12. Select a category that matches the object shown in the video.
  13. Add a description if needed.
  14. Tap Done.
  15. Unlock the phone to show the Quanto Activity page.
  16. Tap through a few different pages and filters to show more of the app.
That’s it.
Step 6

Edit the video

  1. Edit the video using either Edits or CapCut.
  2. Add a hook at the beginning of the video. You can use one from the list below.
  3. Increase the video speed to 1.3x or 1.4x.
  4. If the video includes a description while adding a transaction, speed up that part to 2.5x so viewers do not have to wait while you type.
  5. Add a song.
  6. For Instagram, add this phrase during the last 2 seconds of the video — tap to copy:
  7. Make a copy of the video without the final phrase. This version will be used for TikTok.
Step 7

Post the video

  1. Choose a caption from the list below.
  2. Add 4–5 hashtags from the groups below.
  3. Tag @quanto.tracker in the caption.
  4. Post one video per day on both Instagram and TikTok.
  5. Post between 4 PM and 8 PM.
  6. For TikTok, use the version without the final “Comment guide” phrase.
Videos need to be posted once per day.
